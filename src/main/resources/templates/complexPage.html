About Al Furjan
AI Furjan is located near Jebel Ali Village, Jumeirah Park, and Green Community. It also borders Discovery Gardens and Garden View Villas, which will share the use of a 40 km cycling path. Al Furjan includes 4,000 apartments, villas, townhouses, hotels, and commercial spaces. The area offers a friendly community environment. Units are arranged in small clusters that are connected by promenades and parks. The surrounding landscaped area features 30 park areas and walking paths.
